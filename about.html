About
I'm Victor, a graduate of the York-Sheridan design program (YSDN) and Digital Designer at Interlude Studio. In my diverse work skillset, I use unique ideas to build out creative stories. I love working through the creative process from start to end — seeing how ideas develop and transform into a final product is extremely rewarding.

As a huge Pokémon fan, I spend my free time drawing and making Ditto-faced Pokémon stickers, check them out here or on my Instagram!

One of my favourite hobbies is playing basketball. Every week, I play in a league basketball game to stay active and hone my skills.
